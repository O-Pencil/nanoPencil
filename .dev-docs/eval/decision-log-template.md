# Eval Decision Log Template

> Type: Template
> Status: Draft
> Scope: Recording eval observations, suggestions, and validation outcomes
> Purpose: Prevent analysis discussions from becoming untraceable decisions

## Entry

```markdown
## YYYY-MM-DD - <short title>

Status: proposed | validating | adopted | rejected | deferred
Category: experiment-governance | sal-memory-anchor | tool-usage-analysis | issue-usage
Owner:
Related experiment:
Related issue:
Data window:
Run IDs:
Commit:
Variant:
Model:

### Observation

- What was measured or reviewed?
- Which samples, reports, or artifacts support it?

### Signal

- Is this a one-off observation or repeated pattern?
- What makes the signal strong or weak?

### Suggestion

- What should be changed or tested next?
- Keep this phrased as a proposal, not a final conclusion.

### Risk

- What could go wrong if this suggestion is adopted blindly?

### Validation Plan

- Experiment, replay, review, or test required before adoption.

### Outcome

- Adopted / rejected / deferred.
- Evidence for the final status.
```
